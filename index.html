Scheduling

Project Tracking keeps development rolling

Many projects are completed late because there was no scheduled plan on when milestones and goals should be completed. It is often that things are added to a project and it is important to stay ahead of them before the project tumbles out of control and you are months past your deadline. This is not good for business and can lead to low customer confidence.

By way of communication and active planning projects can be tracked and managed better in order to push them out faster. Scheduling should look at the project as a whole and delve out the longer parts to larger teams that keeps an open line of communication so these parts of the project are completed on time. Smaller portions of the project can be given to single developers. Then bring thos smaller portions together with larger portions at key times in to accomplish a well polished finished project.
